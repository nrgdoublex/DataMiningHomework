Description:
This part is about hypothesis testing. Given U.S census data of obesity rate of each county in year 2004, 2012 and 2013, use hypothesis testing to answer questions.

Contents:
Q1(a).py: Test whether obesity rate across U.S. has increased from 2012 to 2013.
Q1(b).py: Test obesity rate in Tippecanoe County, Indiana is higher than Los Angeles County, California.
Q1(c).py: Compare obesity rate between Los Angeles County, California against Cook County, Illinois and Garfield County, Nebraska against Ohio County, Indiana. And observe their level of certainty.
Q1(d).py: Test whether obesity rate in Tippecanoe County, Indiana has increased from 2012 to 2013.
Q1(e).py: Find all county that are likely to have 1% increase in obesity rate since 2012 to 2013.
Q1(f).py: Find all county that are likely to have some decrease in obesity rate since 2004 to 2013.
Q1(g).py: Find the most obese county in the U.S., using beta prior and maximum a posteriori estimation to simulate.
